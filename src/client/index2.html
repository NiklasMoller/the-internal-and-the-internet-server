Turning the Outside In

Digital art - bringing together an exhibition hall and an online experience

How does it work?

Participants are welcomed to walk around the outside of an exhibition hall. Using their smartphone, they can share personal associations with certain words and upload these on turning-the-outside-in.art



turning-the-outside-in.art then creates an online gallery using the participants' responses. The result can be viewed by all visitors within the exhibition hall.
