Turning the Outside In

Digital art - bringing together an exhibition hall with an online experience

How does it work?

Visitors are welcomed to walk around the outside of an exhibition hall. Using their smartphone, they can share personal associations to certain words and upload these on turning-the-outside-in.art

turning-the-outside-in.art then creates an online gallery using the participants' responses. The result can be viewed by all visitors within the exhibition hall.
